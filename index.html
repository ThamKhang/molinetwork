FAQ

What is Moli Network Partner program?

Answer

Moli Network Partner is a program that allows Creators to make income on Facebook and other platforms such as TikTok, YouTube, Livestream, Website, etc. Also, Creators will be consulted and supported to develop faster with specific tools and benefits

What are the benefits of becoming a Moli Network partner?

Answer

Becoming a member of the Moli Network gives Creators a number of perks:

- Monetize video content on Facebook In-stream Video platform and support monetization on other platforms such as Facebook,TikTok, Livestream, YouTube

- Guaranteed significant increase in revenue.

- Support to turn on some features in the channel/page that you can only see when you go to the Network.

- Supported in-depth analysis for channels and videos, how to optimize channels/videos…

- Videos and content are shared widely throughout the network in the network, helping to build a user community to help optimize channel views and increase revenue.

- Protect your copyrights and help you get revenue from infringing videos whether you are an individual or an organization

- We support you to secure your revenue and make sure you will get paid on time.

- Contact our 24/7 support team for any questions via email, hotline, etc

What are the conditions of becoming a Moli Network partner?

Answer

To be eligible to join our Partnership program on Facebook/TikTok, YouTube, your fanpage should meet the following requirements:

- Monetization eligibility on Facebook Page

- The Fanpage has at least 300,000 monthly views

- Creative and original contents without any copyrights violations

- Adhere to Facebook community guidelines and policies

- If you have good video content and respect for copyright, or have a good channel's real time, inbox us right away, or call the hotline for support on the Network to help you.

How much money can I make?

Answer

Your Revenue depends on:

- How many people are watching your videos and where are they?

- What type of ads are enabled in your videos ?

- Your fan’s behaviours and many other complicated factors.

Why do I need to provide personal information for Moli Network?

Answer

All requested personal information is kept confidential. These are used to process payment transactions, send exclusive gifts or to validate registration.

When will I get the money?

Answer

We will pay you within 25th to the 30th of each month (Net30) of the following month. For example: Your earnings in Jan will be paid during the day 25th-30th of Feb. We will proceed to transfer money if your income exceeds $100 monthly.

I have a lot of views, why didn’t I earn more?

Answer

There are many reasons that impact your earning per view. Your earning depends upon an assortment of factors, surrounding both your content and the audiences consuming that content that matters to advertisers. This includes your country of residency, the type of content you create, your audience method of viewership (Facebook watch page, mobile, embedded, etc) and audience volume.

What should I do if I discover someone else's unauthorized use of my products?

Answer

When detecting cases of violations, posting your products illegally, Moli Network will promptly handle to protect your content copyright and minimize unnecessary copyright disputes. At the same time, Moli Network will guarantee revenue and help you recover lost revenue when your content is infringed by a third party.
